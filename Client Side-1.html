Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register to vote?

Click on the "Register" button and fill in your details including voter ID and email.

Is my vote anonymous?

Yes, our platform ensures complete anonymity and security for all votes submitted.

What if I forget my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it via email.

How can I view results?

Once voting is complete, navigate to the Results page to see live updates.
